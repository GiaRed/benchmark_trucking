Who We Are

Benchmark Trucking is a slinger service company based in Duncan, BC providing gravel, soil and mulch delivery and spreading service to Vancouver Island.

With our Tri-drive slinger truck equipped with a Soil King Extreme lightweight aluminum slinger, we are able to haul 20% more per load than a tandem stone slinger. That’s a free load every 5!  Receive even more value than a conventional soil spreader when you switch from shooting rocks and finish your landscape with soil. With its massive 24-yard soil capacity, our conveyor truck has no rival. 

Operated and dispatched by Spencer Schartner, we offer the best value and service.

Call us today to move your slinging projects to completion!
